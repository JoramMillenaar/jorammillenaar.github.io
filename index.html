As a key member of the software development team at Therapieland B.V., we advanced our e-health platform, providing mental health services in the Netherlands.

Key Responsibilities

  • Developed and maintained large backend systems using Python and Django, alongside efficient database management through Django ORM and PostgreSQL.
  • Managed server operations on GCP and Linux, and handled application deployments with Kubernetes and Docker.
  • Contributed to both the legacy and new platform front-end development using pure HTML/CSS/JS and Vue.js, respectively.

Notable Contributions

  • Led the final phase of a three-year database migration project, employing strategic leadership, practices, and tools to boost productivity. Advocated for and implemented good coding practices including Test-Driven Development (TDD), iterative development, and effective use of AI tools. This led to an impressive 95% test coverage, significantly reducing post-launch issues and stabilizing the project despite its complexity.
  • Optimized a crucial migration script by integrating the Pandas library and optimizing SQL queries, reducing its runtime from two weeks to just 12 hours.
  • Spearheaded software architecture with a focus on modularity and separation of concerns, allowing the project to adapt efficiently without the need for major refactors.
  • Identified and mitigated major cybersecurity vulnerabilities, preventing potential data breaches and addressing several other significant security concerns.

Reflections on Challenges Overcome

  • In my leadership role, I learned the critical importance of maintaining a clear focus and not getting too absorbed in technical details, which can detract from overarching project goals.
  • As someone who values utmost excellence, I've realized the power of iteration and seen how premature optimization can be 'the root of all evil in software development'.
  • While I enjoy tackling challenging problems, I've learned the value of stepping back and seeking help or delegating, which often leads to more effective solutions.
